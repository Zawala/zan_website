Who Are We?

The Zimbabwe AIDS Network (ZAN) was founded in 1992 as a membership-based network with a core mission to coordinate Civil Society Organizations (CSOs) in Zimbabwe in the response to HIV & AIDS, non-communicable diseases, and emerging health challenges. Our primary goal is to ensure that this response is efficient, effective, and impactful across all sectors of society.

Learn More

Our Mission

To improve the health status of Zimbabweans with a special focus on developing, coordinating, and supporting health and community systems strengthening in response to HIV, TB, other non-communicable diseases, and emerging pandemics.

About Us

As the Secretariat for the Civil Society Coordinating Platform on Health, HIV & AIDS, and Sexual and Reproductive Health and Rights (SRHR), ZAN plays a critical role in unifying efforts to improve health outcomes, strengthen capacity within organizations, and advocate for the rights of vulnerable populations.
